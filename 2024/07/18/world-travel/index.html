🌍 New Advanture in 2023

2024-07-18 | world travel | 682 Words | 4 Minutes
Comment

        “Travel around the world” is the most romantic experience that I could imagine in my life. It’s been a while since I completed my world travel in 2023. Whenever I recall this amazing experience, I always feel excited and unforgetable. My body was tanned and my spirit was filled. Now, I think it is a good point to record my experience as my memory.

        Exploration has been the rhythm of my life since I was a kid. My home was moved to different places for multiple times, which enforced me to get familiar with new environment frequently. From a countryside to a small town, from the City of Harbin to Shanghai, a new environment broaden my view and reconstructed my attitude of life. Interestingly, I was a timid boy before middle school. It’s funny to recall my childhood that I could barely go to a library alone which was only 2 kilometer away from my home. To be brave enough, I always went to school as a group with my best friends. I guess that might be caused by frequent changes of living environment. I got easily surprised at unfamiliar stuff. Meanwhile, even if I was too timid to travel alone, I tried my best to hang out with my friends and my family at different places. A huge change happened when I completed my primary school in Harbin. My family moved to Shanghai, an international mega city, not similar with Harbin anymore. The living environment changed again, but this time was a little different. Summer is hotter with higher humity in Shanghai, and people’s accent is different. My old friends were 2,000 kilometers away from me. I had to make new friends, learn new accent, and get used to a more complex environment than before. Fortunately, I enjoyed this change. My parents were busy with work, and I made new friends alone. Also, I got brave enough to travel by myself. Sometimes, I could get some budget from parents to support my trips. My home was moved twice more inside the city until I finished my high school in 2015. I went college in Beijing. Actually, I did not apply any colleges in Shanghai because this time I wanted to explore “ouside” alone. I was not static during undergraduate, and I traveled to most provinces in China. I also learned a lot outside textbook, from travel and work in the Red Cross. Then, I applied UIUC for my Masters’ Degrees. In terms of my expertise, I broaded my knowledge from civil engineering to urban plannning to geography. I enjoyed merging my knowledge and utilized my advange to guide new field, such as use data analysis in policy and decision making. When completed my degrees. I enrolled in a post-graduate program, which allowed me to work in the government of Illinois, and make enough money for travelling. At the age of 25, I got a perfect condition with good “health”, flexable “schedule”, and enough “budget”. Even I could have applied a higher-salary job, but I still decided to explore the world first and learn from the “outside”.

        On July 18th one year ago (2023), as the horn of Amtrak coming close to me at Springfield train station, I carried on my bags and started my expected new advanture of my life.

        Why do I post these story one year later?

        A: While traveling, too many stories and new stuff ocupied my mind, I could only write them down and record them by phone. Now, I have built my own blog website and been back to “normal” life rhythm, which allows to have a place to hold my stories and memorize them again. What is more important is that the experience will remind me to keep exploring the world even if I step into the next chapter or I am merged into “ordinary life”. I will keep working hard and learning from the world again and again.
